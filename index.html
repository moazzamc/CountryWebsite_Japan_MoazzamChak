About Japan

Japan is an island nation in East Asia known for its unique culture, advanced technology, beautiful landscapes, and rich history. From traditional temples to modern skyscrapers, Japan blends the old and new in a fascinating way.

Learn more on the official tourism website: Japan Travel Guide

Famous Cities

  • Tokyo – Japan’s bustling capital.
  • Kyoto – Known for temples and geisha culture.
  • Osaka – Famous for food and nightlife.

Culture

Japanese culture is known worldwide for traditions such as tea ceremonies, kimono, festivals, anime, and martial arts. Japan also has a strong influence on global technology, cuisine, and art.
